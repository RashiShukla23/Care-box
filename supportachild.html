The Orphan Children Care Project

India has over 30 million orphaned and abandoned children, many living in extreme poverty without access to education, healthcare, or proper nutrition. Our campaign provides comprehensive support through education scholarships, nutritious meals, healthcare services, school supplies, hygiene products, and clothing. Every ₹1,500 sponsors one child's education for a month, including tuition, books, and tutoring. ₹1,200 provides a month's nutritious meals. ₹1,000 gives a child proper clothing for a season. Join us in giving these children not just survival, but a chance to thrive, dream, and build a better future through education and care.
