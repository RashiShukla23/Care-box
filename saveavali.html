The Aravalli Conservation Project

The Aravalli Range, India's oldest mountain range, is facing severe degradation due to mining, deforestation, and urban expansion. This ancient ecosystem is home to unique biodiversity, acts as a crucial water reservoir, and prevents desertification in North India. Our campaign focuses on restoring degraded areas, planting native species, empowering local communities as conservation stewards, and creating sustainable livelihoods through agroforestry. Every contribution helps us protect this vital ecological barrier that affects the climate and water security of over 50 million people.
